This is a project that I made like a test for BBVA bank. The challenge was to address a highly relevant social problem: 𝘂𝗻𝗪𝗮𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗱 𝗹𝗼𝗻𝗲𝗹𝗶𝗻𝗲𝘀𝘀 in young people.

📣 This reality affects 𝟭/𝟰 young people between the ages of 16 and 29, and the most alarming thing is that 82% of those who suffer from it indicate that its origin lies in not having felt part of their community during childhood.

📑 To try to alleviate this social problem, I designed a solution in the form of a mobile app, called 𝗛𝗼𝗺𝗲 𝗕𝗼𝗻𝗲𝘀, which has ludic-therapeutic functions, based on a virtual assistant with artificial intelligence that allows a dynamic and personalized interaction.

The methodological approach was the real protagonist. I used the DesignThinking framework, making sure to go deep into each stage of the process.


This project allowed me to reinforce my commitment to 𝗽𝗲𝗼𝗽𝗹𝗲-𝗰𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗲𝗱 𝗱𝗲𝘀𝗶𝗴𝗻. In just two pages (16:9 format), I managed to capture how the method and strategic decisions guided the entire process.
