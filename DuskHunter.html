Game´s Description


When night falls in the Forest Palace, a small Spirit goes hunting. The light is strongest in the dark, as evidenced by the mysterious lantern that carries the power of light.

Take control of the Spirit and use your lantern to make your way through the halls of the great Forest Palace.

Unleash the power of its light and change its color to adapt to the different dangers lurking inside.

Stop projectiles at the right moment, dodge, and unleash your fury against your enemies in this action game, where every game is different, and make use of your skill to squeeze a dynamic combat system that demands precision and reflexes.

My roles during development

  • UI/UX Designer and Programmer: I designed and programmed the interface present during the game, as well as the clock that drives the day-night cycle. Also design the feedback given to the player after different inputs.
  • Level Designer and Programmer: I programmed the procedural generation
  • Game Design: Together with the other game designer, we designed the idea of the game, its rules and all the mechanics in it.
  • 2D Artist: I made the artwork for the game interface, the clock, the healthbar and the lamp color change indicator.
  • SFX Artist: I made the sound effects for the game and placed them in such a way as to provide correct feedback to the player.
  • Tester: I tested the game in its different development versions to find minor and major bugs.
