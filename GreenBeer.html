GREEN BEER
ACCESSIBLE BEER FOR THE PEOPLE

  1. Home

🍺 𝗚𝗿𝗲𝗲𝗻𝗕𝗲𝗲𝗿 — The smoothest, most accessible and delightful way to order at your favorite beer garden 🌿

Picture this: you arrive at an open-air bar, the sun is warm, the vibe is just right… and you don’t have to wave, wait, or stress to get a drink. You simply open GreenBeer, scan a QR code on your table or connect by proximity to the bar, choose your beer, and that’s it — order placed, payment processed, you just relax and enjoy.

GreenBeer isn’t here to save the planet — but it is here to save your time.

Here’s what makes it special:

  • 🌿 A nature-inspired visual design that feels fresh, calm, and trustworthy.
  • 📱 A foolproof user experience, packed with safety nets: clear alerts, double-check confirmations, and a smooth, guided flow.
  • 🧠 Accessibility features built-in: don’t feel like using the app? Just tap a button to call the waiter.
  • 💳 A personal digital account that takes care of payment automatically — no need to pull out your wallet or split bills.

🌍 GreenBeer is more than an app — it's a tailored solution for open-air beer gardens, designed to grow and adapt. Built for today’s casual, outdoor bars, with a clear path to expand into other hospitality spaces tomorrow.
